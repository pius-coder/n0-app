
# Design Workflow

## Section Anatomy
Every landing page section follows:
1. Eyebrow (optional) — small badge or label above title
2. Title — one powerful headline
3. Subtitle — one sentence explaining
4. Content — the actual visual content
5. CTA (optional) — action button

## Page Flow for Marketplace
1. Hero — hook + primary CTA
2. Social proof — trust signals (numbers or logos)
3. How it works — reduce friction (3 steps)
4. Featured items — show the product
5. Categories — help users self-select
6. Benefits — why us vs competitors
7. Testimonials — peer validation
8. Final CTA — last push to convert
9. FAQ — handle objections

## Alternating Pattern
- Section 1: white bg, centered
- Section 2: neutral-50 bg
- Section 3: white bg
- Section 4: neutral-950 bg (dark, dramatic)
- Repeat pattern for visual rhythm
